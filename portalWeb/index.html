Our Facilities

our facilities are designed to support the holistic development of our students, providing them with the resources and
opportunities they need to thrive academically,socially, and emotionally during their time with us.

HTA LIBRARY

The HTA Library provides access to digital resources, including e-books, online databases, and multimedia resources, further expanding the scope of knowledge available to our students.

HTA BT GYM

The HTA BT Gym is a state-of-the-art fitness facility dedicated to promoting health, wellness, and physical fitness among students, faculty, and staff. Situated on our school campus, it serves as a hub for exercise, training, and recreational activities, fostering a vibrant and active community.

HTA Audio Visual Room (AVR)

AVR is a dynamic space within our school campus dedicated to enhancing the learning experience through multimedia technology and interactive presentations.
